The advances in digital technologies are rapid, powerful and disruptive. They have a strong impact on all areas of our lives and increasingly change how society works. A lot of these aspects and transformations are opaque and complex, carrying the risk of widening existing social gaps and inequalities. But digital technology can also be a means to produce new nodes of meaning and beauty, and be conceived as a tool for everyone to create and express themselves. Art has the strength of instigating new narratives, of revealing new levels of perception, of giving a tangible experience to the invisible facets of the digital realms. Bringing it outdoors in a setting accessible to all, and embedding it in the urban infrastructure further extends the scope and effect of its outreach. It not only becomes a public centre of interest and a source of inspiration for those seeking for it, but also provides delight and food for thought to the ones discovering them by chance. Digital Icon Zurich is in the process of designing an international call for projects. It will ask people from all around the world to propose ideas for an iconic public artwork that has the power to open dialogue and engage the city and its citizens, to shift the notion of digital technology towards one of an enabling and creative tool for humanity. The awarded project will be materialised and become part of the city’s landscape. Digital Icon Zurich is in the process of designing an international call for projects.
